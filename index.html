About me

I am a maverick ML, DL researcher and a Software Engineer (at RedDot Digital Limited) by profession.

I have done my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET) in April, 2022. My undergraduate thesis was about detecting abnormalities from musculoskeletal radiographs, where I employed deep learning techniques under the guidance of Md. Kamrul Hasan and Md. Masum Al Mesba. Again, with Md. Kamrul Hasan sir, I completed another research work on COVID-19 where we proposed a 3D Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)-based classification approach considering both the inter-and intra-slice spatial voxel information. Previously, I also worked with Mr. Sunanda Das sir on sentence generation using LSTM based deep learning.

My research interests include (but not limited to) the followings:

  • Deep Learning
  • Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Human Computer Interaction

Check out my Github page.
